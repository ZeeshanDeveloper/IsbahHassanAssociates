About Us

Translating visionary concepts into concrete realities
Since 1996

Isbah Hassan & Associates offer elegant and innovative design solutions to meet contemporary requirements. Users derive as much pleasure from occupying their buildings as we do in designing them. A holistic approach ensures that buildings are stylish, practical, comfortable and in perfect harmony with their environment. Mindful of developments that influence 21st century architecture, we opt for stylized, space efficient and financially viable buildings, designed by innovative thought and respect for the environment.

We believe in functional elegance and responsiveness to a site’s physical surroundings keeping in perspective the brief of the client and the approved budget constraints. This formula has transpired into several successful developments that now line an emerging modern landscape in Pakistan -and we are proud to be part of this renaissance.

Our Services

Architecture

We conceive and realize developments that influence 21st-century architecture. Our projects reflect our flair for stylistic elements and designs fueled by a unique vision for the seamless incorporation of efficient spacing. We move with a focus on creating state of the art yet livable spaces blended with sustainability and financial viability.

interiors

Our holistic approach to interior design seamlessly blends comfort, practicality, and aesthetics. We believe in building a functional space that rests on the pillars of elegance and intentional sophistication. Every element balances in perfect harmony with the architecture and compliments a modern lifestyle naturally.

Planing

We have developed a sophisticated and thorough approach to design through impeccable planning. We design a vision for your new lifestyle and bring it to life. Whether it is a drastic overhaul or minute renovations, our purposeful planning takes into consideration the durability of your space to ensure it stays relevant for decades.
