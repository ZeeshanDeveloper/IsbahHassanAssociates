About isbah hassan & associates

About

we turn ideas into works of art.

Isbah Hassan & Associates offer elegant and innovative design solutions to meet contemporary requirements. Users derive as much pleasure from occupying their buildings as we do in designing them. A holistic approach ensures that buildings are stylish,practical, comfortable and in perfect harmony with their environment. Mindful of developments that influence 21st century architecture, we opt for stylized, space efficient and financially viable buildings, designed by innovative thought and respect for the environment. We believe in functional elegance and responsiveness to a site’s physical surroundings keeping in perspective the brief of the client and the approved budget constraints.
